About Us

At DAP, we are passionate about preserving the environment and dedicate our efforts to protecting protected areas against deforestation. Our team is made up of professionals committed to biodiversity conservation and sustainable development.

Problems

Deforestation of protected areas is a serious threat to biodiversity, caused by agriculture, logging and urbanization. Raising awareness about the importance of forests is crucial. The population can help by spreading information and choosing sustainable products.

Consequences

The consequences of this deforestation are on a global level, it can cause pollution to the air and the environment of animals and plants. With deforestation the air will become more polluted and the temperature will increase and we will end up in a totally polluted world, causing humanity to die.

Technology

Drones that will scan the areas where they are being protected and if it is analyzed that deforestation is taking place there, the nearest police station is informed to intervene in the deforestation and after the area is released, robots are sent to replant the lost trees
